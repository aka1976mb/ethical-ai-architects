# Code of Conduct for Ethical AI Architects

We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and constructive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

*   **Be Kind:** We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
*   **Be Rigorous:** Critique arguments with evidence and clarity, focusing on the content of discussions.
*   **Be Open:** Invite and respect diverse perspectives and experiences. Listen actively and empathetically.

## Unacceptable Behaviors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

*   Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, age, race, ethnicity, nationality, ancestry, religion, or lack thereof, or other protected characteristics.
*   Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following.
*   Unwanted photography or recording.
*   Sustained disruption of discussions or other events.
*   Inappropriate physical contact.
*   Unwelcome sexual attention.
*   Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behavior.

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please report it by:

*   **Contacting:** [Insert email address or reporting form link here, e.g., cofc@ethicalaia.org]
*   **Process:** Reports will be acknowledged promptly, reviewed fairly, and resolutions will be documented. All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Thank you for helping us create a positive community.
