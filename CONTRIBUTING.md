# Contributing to Ethical AI Architects

We welcome contributions from everyone! By participating in this project, you agree to abide by our Code of Conduct.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Read the Docs:** Familiarize yourself with our Vision, Roadmap, and Governance.
2.  **Find an Issue:** Look for issues labeled "good first issue" or propose a new one.
3.  **Discuss:** Align on the scope of your contribution before opening a Pull Request.

## Standards

*   **Documentation-first:** Every change should come with clear documentation or examples.
*   **Reproducibility:** Provide instructions, seeds, and data notes for any research or tooling.
*   **Ethical Diligence:** Note potential risks, mitigations, and open questions related to ethical impact.

## Pull Request Checklist

*   **Purpose:** Clearly state what problem your PR solves.
*   **Scope:** Summarize the changes made.
*   **Evidence:** Include tests, examples, or references to support your changes.
*   **Ethics Note:** Detail any potential ethical impacts or trade-offs.
*   **Review Ready:** Ensure your commits are clear, small, and cohesive.

Thank you for contributing!
