# Taxonomy of Ethical AI Dimensions

This document outlines a preliminary taxonomy of ethical dimensions relevant to AI systems, particularly foundational models. This taxonomy will evolve with research and community input.

## Core Dimensions

*   **Fairness:** Addressing bias, discrimination, and equitable outcomes.
*   **Transparency & Interpretability:** Understanding how AI systems make decisions.
*   **Accountability:** Establishing responsibility for AI system impacts.
*   **Safety & Robustness:** Ensuring reliable and secure operation, minimizing harm.
*   **Privacy:** Protecting sensitive information and user data.
*   **Human Oversight & Control:** Maintaining meaningful human agency over AI systems.
*   **Sustainability:** Considering environmental and societal resource consumption.
*   **Beneficence:** Promoting positive societal impact and well-being.
